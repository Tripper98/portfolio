Who I Am

I'm a GenAI & NLP Engineer currently at IPSOS France, where I build production-grade agentic AI systems — multi-agent pipelines, RAG architectures, and multimodal LLM services — on Azure and GCP.

My work lives at the intersection of research and engineering: translating state-of-the-art LLM capabilities into reliable, observable, and scalable systems. I care about the full stack — from embedding strategy and chunking to CI/CD, containerisation, and LLMOps monitoring.

Prior to IPSOS, I worked at Henceforth (Rabat) on diffusion model pipelines, custom ASR systems, and edge AI optimisation — cutting inference latency by 30% using TensorRT and ONNX.

Educated at Claude Bernard Lyon 1 University (Master's in AI) and ENSA Morocco (Engineering in Big Data). Three-time winner of the Huawei ICT Competition Cloud Track, including 1st Prize at the Global Final in Dongguan, China.
